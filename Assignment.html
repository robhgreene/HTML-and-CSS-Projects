Crash Nebula

This is the story of Crash Nebula. For many years, Crash Nebula traveled the cosmos to fight evil and aid thoes in need.

Crash Nebula grew up on the planet Praxis. He spent his youth on a farm. He enjoyed spending time with his family and helping out anyway he could. As he grew older, he longed to take to the stars and seek adventure. Eventually, he would get his chance.

Chapter 2: The Academy

Crash Neblua was accepted to the prestigious Space Ranger Academy. Through hardwork, whit and charisma, Crash made a name for himself and quickly set himself apart for his peers. Crash saw the value in being a team player and helped his fellow students reach their full potential. Graduation finally came, and Crash was off to his first assignment.

Chapter 3: The Rim

The outer rim proved to be a challege for Crash. All of his training had served him well. Each day he risked life and limb in his duty as a Space Ranger. The peace was kept, but an uneasying feeling of encroaching doom whispered in the back of his mind.

Epilogue

After years in the Space Rangers, Crash felt there was something more waiting for him. He assembled a team of his most trusted companions. Soon, they would set out beyond the known expanse, and into the unknown.
